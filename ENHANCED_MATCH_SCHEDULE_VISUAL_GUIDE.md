## ?? DATA FLOW DIAGRAM

```
Server JSON
    ?
    ?? scheduled_time ? ScheduledTime (UTC)
    ?? predicted_time ? PredictedTime (UTC)
    ?? red_alliance ? RedAlliance ("1,2,3")
  ?? blue_alliance ? BlueAlliance ("4,5,6")
?? red_score ? RedScore (nullable)
    ?? blue_score ? BlueScore (nullable)
    
    ? Processing

Match Model (Computed Properties)
    ?
 ?? TimeDisplay
    ?   ?? PredictedTime ?? ScheduledTime
    ?       ?? Convert to LocalTime
    ? ?? Format "h:mm tt"
    ?
    ?? DateDisplay
  ?   ?? PredictedTime ?? ScheduledTime
    ?       ?? Convert to LocalTime
    ?           ?? Format "ddd, MMM d"
    ?
    ?? RedTeams
    ?   ?? RedAlliance.Replace(",", ", ")
    ?
    ?? BlueTeams
    ?   ?? BlueAlliance.Replace(",", ", ")
    ?
  ?? IsInProgress
    ?   ?? Time <= Now && Time + 10min >= Now
    ?
    ?? IsCompleted
    ?   ?? RedScore != null || BlueScore != null
    ?
    ?? IsUpcoming
        ?? Time > Now

    ? Binding

XAML Display
    ?
    ?? Time Column ? TimeDisplay, DateDisplay
    ?? Status Dot ? IsInProgress
    ?? Status Badge ? IsInProgress, IsCompleted
    ?? Alliance Display ? RedTeams, BlueTeams
    ?? Score Display ? RedScore, BlueScore
```
